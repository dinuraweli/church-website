Smart Technology

The new Honda Civic is packed with extra features that makes your drive more interesting.

Fully digital driver's display

A 10.2” digital driver’s display comes as standard – situated behind the steering wheel. You can select the information you want to see via controls on the multifunctional steering wheel.

Immersive audio

With a premium 12 speaker BOSE sound system on selected grades, you can enjoy crystal-clear audio on every journey.

Panoramic glass roof

The CR-V’s panoramic glass roof lets sunlight flood the interior, making every journey feel bright, spacious and refreshing.

Heated leather steering wheel

Enjoy the luxurious feel of a full leather heated steering wheel on those cold winter mornings. Available in selected grades.

Smart headlights

With Adaptive Driving Beam, available on top grades, the CR-V automatically adjusts the headlights – allowing you to see the road ahead without dazzling others. Plus, with LED front headlights, it’s as effective in foggy conditions as standard fog lights.

Easy access ISO FIX points

With easily accessible ISO FIX fittings (I-SIZE) across the back seats, and ample space for both forward and rear facing seats, you can ensure your child is always comfortable and secure.
